Georginify settings

This extension help you to learn Georgian aplphabet mkhedruli (მხედრული) by replacing similarly sounding letters of English and Russian alphabets with Georgian letters. Here you can customize which letters to replace since replacing many letters at once is diffuctilt to read.

For some georgian letters there are no direct sound in english or russian alphabets so there are multiple options for your choice. You can select it depending on the combination of other letters you are learning at the moment.

Select what letters to replace in the list below. We recommend to learn a few letters at once.
